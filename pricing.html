Frequently asked questions

💸 Are there any monthly fees?

No. All plans are one‑time payments. You only pay for domain renewal ($12‑15/year) if you don’t already own one. Hosting is included free for the first year.

⚡ How long does it take to launch?

Starter & Pro sites launch in 5‑7 business days. Enterprise projects take 10‑14 days, depending on customizations.

🔧 Can I update the website myself?

Absolutely. We provide a simple CMS dashboard (or Framer/WordPress) with video training. You can edit text, images, and add projects anytime.

📈 Do you help with SEO?

Yes. Pro and Enterprise include local SEO setup (meta tags, Google Business Profile linking, keyword research). We also give you a content guide for ranking higher.

🛠️ What if I need more pages later?

You can upgrade anytime. Additional pages are $25/page for Starter/Pro, or we’ll apply the difference toward an Enterprise package.

🎯 Is there a money‑back guarantee?

100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not thrilled within 14 days of launch, we'll refund every cent — no questions asked.
